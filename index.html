Lace Up for the Elgin 5K Fundraiser

The first ever Elgin 5K Fundraiser is sprinting into action on Saturday May 17th -- help fuel the world-class Elgin ISD Parent Teacher Association while enjoying a lively race starting at Neidig Elementary School, past Austin Community College campus, and through Shenandoah green belt. Open to all ages, this family-friendly event features a scenic 5K, post race snacks, awesome prizes, and a free Kiddie Fun Run for little runners (12 and under).


Check in: 6:30 AM at Neidig Elementary

Runners Lineup at Start Line: 7:00 AM

5K Run Start: 7:20 AM

Kid's Fun Run (FREE!): 9:00 AM


Vendor Fair: 7 AM - 12 PM
